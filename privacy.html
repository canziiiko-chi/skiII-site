skiII Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

skiII is a local-first card note app built around the idea of capturing first and organizing later. We value privacy, local ownership, and clear data handling.

中文

1. 核心原则

skiII 不需要注册账号即可使用。

skiII 不提供开发者服务器账号系统，也不会主动将你的卡片、灵感、图片、录音、链接内容或导入文件上传到开发者服务器。

你在 skiII 中创建和保存的内容默认保存在你的设备本地，包括但不限于：

  • 灵感气泡
  • 卡片与序号卡片
  • 图片、录音、链接内容
  • 分类、标签、回收站数据
  • 导入、导出和备份文件

开发者无法直接访问、查看、复制或分析你保存在本地设备中的笔记内容。

2. 本地数据存储

skiII 的主要数据保存在你的设备本地。你可以通过 App 内导入、导出或备份功能主动管理自己的数据。

如果你删除 App，iOS 可能会同时删除 App 本地数据。建议你在需要保留数据时，使用 skiII 提供的导出或备份功能自行保存。

3. iCloud 同步（可选，Beta）

skiII 提供可选的 iCloud 同步功能（完整版功能，当前为 Beta）。开启后，你的卡片、灵感气泡、分类和附件会以文件形式保存到你自己的 Apple 账号下的 iCloud 云盘容器中，用于在你的多台设备之间同步。

  • 同步数据存储在你的 iCloud 账号中，由 Apple 的 iCloud 服务处理，受 Apple 的隐私政策和 iCloud 条款约束。
  • 开发者没有服务器参与同步过程，也无法访问、查看你 iCloud 中的同步内容。
  • 你可以随时在 App 设置中关闭同步；关闭后本地数据不受影响。
  • 删除 App 不会删除 iCloud 中已同步的数据；你可以在 iOS 设置的 iCloud 储存空间管理中查看或删除它们。
  • 同步内容计入你的 iCloud 储存空间配额。

4. 系统权限的使用

为了提供特定功能，skiII 可能会请求以下系统权限。所有权限仅在你使用对应功能时触发。

  • 相机权限：用于拍照、识别文字或为卡片添加图片。
  • 相册权限：用于从相册选择图片，或将导出的长图保存到相册。
  • 麦克风权限：用于录音功能。录音文件默认保存在你的设备本地。
  • 语音识别权限：用于将录音内容转换为文字。相关处理可能受 Apple 系统服务和隐私政策约束。
  • 位置权限：仅当你主动为卡片片段记录当前位置时使用。
  • 通知权限：用于序号卡片提醒。
  • Face ID / Touch ID 权限：用于隐私锁。生物识别数据由 iOS 系统处理，skiII 不会获取或保存你的面容、指纹等生物识别数据。

5. 链接读取与第三方网站

当你在 skiII 中读取网页链接、公众号、小红书、微博、X（Twitter）、Bluesky 或 AI 对话分享链接时，skiII 可能会从你的设备向对应网站发起网络请求，以获取网页标题、正文、图片或公开可见内容，并将其整理为卡片。

部分需要登录的网站支持在 App 内置网页中打开：你在内置网页中输入的登录凭据由 iOS 系统的网页组件（WKWebView）保存和管理，skiII 不会读取、收集或上传你的账号密码；提取操作只读取当前页面可见的内容。

这些请求由你的设备发起。目标网站可能会接收到网络请求相关信息，例如 IP 地址、浏览器环境或访问时间。第三方网站的数据处理方式不受本隐私政策约束，请参考对应网站的隐私政策。

skiII 不会将读取到的链接内容上传到开发者服务器。

6. 分享扩展

当你从其他 App 分享文字、图片或链接到 skiII 时，系统会将你主动分享的内容交给 skiII 处理。skiII 只会保存你主动确认或导入的内容。

7. 购买与 TestFlight

skiII 可能使用 Apple 提供的 App Store / StoreKit 能力处理购买或一次买断。付款信息、订单处理和退款由 Apple 负责，skiII 不会获取你的银行卡或完整支付信息。

如果你通过 TestFlight 测试 skiII，Apple 可能会收集并向开发者提供崩溃日志、诊断信息、安装状态和测试反馈，用于改进 App 稳定性和体验。这些数据由 Apple 的 TestFlight 服务处理。

8. 反馈与联系

如果你通过邮件、小红书或其他方式主动联系开发者，你提供的联系方式、反馈内容、截图或说明将仅用于回复问题、定位故障和改进产品。

App 设置中提供「导出诊断包」功能：由你主动导出并选择是否发送，内容包含设备型号、系统版本、App 版本、数据规模统计（数量，不含内容）、同步开关状态和 iOS 系统回传的崩溃记录，不包含任何笔记正文、图片或录音。skiII 不会自动上传诊断数据。

9. 未成年人隐私

skiII 不主动收集用户个人信息，也不面向未成年人收集个人数据。如果你认为有未成年人向我们提供了个人信息，请通过上述联系方式联系我们。

10. 政策更新

如果 skiII 后续新增会影响数据处理方式的功能，我们会更新本隐私政策，并在必要时通过 App、官网或 App Store 页面说明变更。

English

1. Core principles

skiII can be used without creating an account.

skiII does not provide a developer-operated account system and does not intentionally upload your cards, inspirations, images, recordings, link content, or imported files to a developer server.

Content you create and save in skiII is stored locally on your device by default, including but not limited to:

  • inspiration bubbles
  • cards and numbered card steps
  • images, audio recordings, and link content
  • categories, tags, and trash data
  • imported, exported, and backup files

The developer cannot directly access, view, copy, or analyze the note content stored locally on your device.

2. Local data storage

skiII stores its main data locally on your device. You can manage your own data through import, export, and backup features in the app.

If you delete the app, iOS may also delete the app's local data. We recommend using skiII's export or backup features if you need to keep your data.

3. iCloud sync (optional, Beta)

skiII offers optional iCloud sync (a full-version feature, currently in Beta). When enabled, your cards, inspiration bubbles, categories, and attachments are stored as files in your own Apple account's iCloud Drive container and synced across your devices.

  • Synced data lives in your iCloud account, is processed by Apple's iCloud service, and is governed by Apple's privacy policy and iCloud terms.
  • No developer server is involved in syncing; the developer cannot access or view your synced content in iCloud.
  • You can turn sync off at any time in the app settings; your local data is unaffected.
  • Deleting the app does not delete synced data in iCloud; you can review or delete it in iOS Settings under iCloud storage management.
  • Synced content counts toward your iCloud storage quota.

4. System permissions

skiII may request the following system permissions only when you use related features:

  • Camera: used for taking photos, text recognition, or adding images to cards.
  • Photo Library: used for selecting images or saving exported long images.
  • Microphone: used for audio recording. Recordings are stored locally by default.
  • Speech Recognition: used to transcribe recordings into text. This may be processed by Apple system services and subject to Apple's privacy policy.
  • Location: used only when you choose to record your current location for a card step.
  • Notifications: used for local reminders on card steps.
  • Face ID / Touch ID: used for the privacy lock feature. Biometric data is handled by iOS; skiII does not receive or store your face or fingerprint data.

5. Link reading and third-party websites

When you ask skiII to read a web page, public account article, Xiaohongshu post, Weibo post, X (Twitter) post, Bluesky post, or AI conversation share link, skiII may request the corresponding website from your device to obtain visible content such as titles, text, images, or public conversation content.

Some sites that require login can be opened inside the app's built-in web page: any credentials you enter there are stored and managed by the iOS web component (WKWebView); skiII does not read, collect, or upload your account credentials, and extraction only reads content visible on the current page.

These requests are made from your device. The destination website may receive network request information such as IP address, browser environment, or access time. Third-party websites are governed by their own privacy policies.

skiII does not upload the retrieved link content to a developer server.

6. Share extension

When you share text, images, or links from another app to skiII, the system passes the content you choose to share to skiII. skiII only saves content that you intentionally confirm or import.

7. Purchases and TestFlight

skiII may use Apple's App Store / StoreKit services to process purchases or one-time unlocks. Payment information, order processing, and refunds are handled by Apple. skiII does not receive your bank card information or complete payment details.

If you test skiII through TestFlight, Apple may collect and provide crash logs, diagnostics, installation status, and tester feedback to the developer to improve stability and user experience. This data is handled by Apple's TestFlight service.

8. Feedback and contact

If you contact the developer by email, Xiaohongshu, or another channel, the contact information, feedback, screenshots, or descriptions you provide will be used only to respond to your request, diagnose issues, and improve the product.

The app settings include an "Export diagnostics bundle" feature. It is exported by you and shared only if you choose to. It contains device model, OS version, app version, data-size statistics (counts only, no content), sync toggle state, and crash records reported by iOS. It never includes note text, images, or recordings. skiII never uploads diagnostics automatically.

Contact: menchook@163.com; Xiaohongshu: ganchi.

9. Children's privacy

skiII does not intentionally collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us using the information above.

10. Changes to this policy

If skiII adds features that materially change how data is handled, we will update this Privacy Policy and, when necessary, notify users through the app, website, or App Store page.
